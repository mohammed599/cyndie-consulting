REASONS WHY YOUR FITNESS BUSINESS SHOULD BE SUCCESSFUL

Physical activity is intrinsic to wellness, and the link between physical activity and good health has been clinically and scientifically proven.  Yet, physical activity has been declining in the past few decades.

There is no question that our modern lifestyles discourage physical activity.   Practically all aspects of life – from work and home chores to socialization, shopping, entertainment, etc. – have been transformed by urbanization, technology, motorized transportation, and new business models to become ever more sedentary.

Not surprisingly, most people now need to schedule time and activities in order to stay physically active.  

The global growth of the fitness industry plays an important role in expanding access to physical activity, by making exercise more convenient, affordable, fun, personalized, and results-driven.

Health clubs and gyms have experienced tremendous global expansion over the last decade, with over 50% growth in revenues from 2007-2018 at 595$ billion.

Among the overworked, sleep-deprived, and anxiety-ridden, as well as those who suffer from chronic pain and disease, many are seeking relief in exercise and physical activities.

As people have become more sedentary, they  now treat physical activity as an intentional health-maintenance routine thus the importance of the encompassing fitness industries.

The various gyms need to appeal to its consumers' needs so as to retain clients and  grow as a business.  Understanding your clients needs puts you in a good position as a fitness business to work on your approach and get to have return customers.

Knowing your gym audience and what makes your gym special over the competition will help you use the correct verbiage when marketing your gym.

Fitness is a billion dollar market for a reason. Most people want to keep away from health insurance costs and new demand for healthy foods leads to fitness too.

With the concepts of wellness and mindfulness becoming ever more important for consumers, fitness has become a more relevant goal for many people, putting it in a good position to meet these needs.
